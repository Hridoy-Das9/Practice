  1. Saint-Martin

    Saint Martin's Island, located in the Bay of Bengal, is a small yet enchanting coral island in Bangladesh. A tour to this tropical paradise offers a unique blend of natural beauty, tranquility, and adventure. The journey typically begins with a scenic boat ride from Teknaf, presenting breathtaking views of the turquoise sea and distant horizon. Upon arrival, visitors are greeted by pristine sandy beaches, crystal-clear waters, and swaying palm trees. The island's vibrant marine life, including colorful coral reefs and exotic fish, makes it a haven for snorkeling and diving enthusiasts. Evening walks along the beach are a serene experience, with the soothing sound of waves and a sky illuminated by countless stars.

    Beyond its natural allure, Saint Martin's Island also provides a taste of local culture and cuisine. Freshly caught seafood, especially the island's signature lobsters and crabs, is a must-try for food lovers.
